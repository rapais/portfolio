Automatic Fire Extenguisher

  • Uses: Esp 32, Fire sensor, Servo, TinkerCAD, Firebase, Node-RED
  • Project date: 2022
  • Project URL: Drive

About

One of the CEP-CCIT IoT projects that me and my team work on, an implementation of Esp 32. Work when the fire sensor detect a high heat near it, and then shoot the water to the position of the heat and send it to the Firebase to give text output and Node-RED to give an image output.
